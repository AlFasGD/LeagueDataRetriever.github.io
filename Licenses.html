Licensing


There are three types of purchasable licenses:

Type Price
3-month $6
1-year $15
All-time $30

Expirable licenses can be stacked, where the most recently purchased will be applied last. For instance, first buying a 3-month license and then an 1-year one will result in first applying the 3-month license. Stacked licenses can be refunded up to 2 times per account. The all-time license cannot be stacked on top of other licenses; if another license is already in use, the all-time will immediately replace the current license.
